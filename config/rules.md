# Rulebook

---

## General Rules

- The fantasy league consists of **three divisions**: Premier League, Championship, and League One *(12 teams per division)*
- **Promotion & Relegation:** Top **2** teams promoted, bottom **2** teams relegated
- If a promoted team does not renew, the next-best team in the division below is promoted
- Rosters are selected via a **snake-format draft**
- **Head-to-head scoring:** Win = 3pts · Draw = 1pt · Loss = 0pts
- The season includes **38 Game Weeks**

## League Cup

- Interleague knockout tournament starting **January**
- **12 groups of 3 teams** — one team from each division, drawn randomly
- Each team plays the other two once in the group stage

| Group Finish | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Best 8 group winners | Auto-qualify for Round of 16 |
| Remaining 4 winners + 12 runners-up | Playoff Round |
| Bottom team in each group | Eliminated |

- All knockout rounds are **two legs**, including the playoff round
- **Aggregate score** determines the winner

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|

| Time per pick | 1:30 |
| Trade picks | Not allowed |

## Game Week Rosters

- Max **11 active players** per game week
- Players **lock** 1 minute before their individual match begins
- Players gain new positional eligibility after **4+ games** in that position

### Formation Rules

| Position | Min | Max |
|---|---|---|
| GK | 1 | 1 |
| DEF | 3 | 5 |
| MID | 3 | 5 |
| FWD | 1 | 3 |

---

## Injured Reserve (IR)

- Each team has **1 IR slot**
- Player must have a **red injury flag** to be placed on IR
- When they return to fitness, they must be removed from IR within **2 game weeks**
- Failure to do so **blocks all claims and trades**

---

## Auto-Sub System

- Set your bench priority order before the GW deadline
- Once the first match starts, priority locks
- If a starter doesn't play, the highest-ranked eligible bench player is subbed in — positional rules must still be met
- Auto-subs run **~1 hour after the final match** of the game week

**Example:** If MID is first in priority but a FWD misses their match, the MID does **not** sub in if it would exceed 5 MIDs.

---

## Waiver Wire (FAAB)

| Rule | Detail |
|---|---|
| Season budget | $100 |
| Minimum bid | $0 |
| Processing times | Mondays & Thursdays at 10 PM ET |
| Winning bid | Highest bid wins |
| Tie resolution | Waiver priority order |
| Using waivers | Sends your team to the back of the priority line |
| New additions | Must stay on waivers at least 48 hours |

- **Orange "add" icon** = waiver player
- **Green "add" icon** = free agent

---

## Free Agents

- Can be added anytime before their match begins
- Become locked to waivers once their game starts
- Revert to waivers at next processing time

---

## Trades

- Trades allowed until the **start of Game Week 29**
- After the first match of GW29 → **trade window closes**
- FAAB may be traded
- Trades flagged as collusion or fraudulent will be reviewed and reversed

---

## Standings Tiebreakers

| Priority | Tiebreaker |
|---|---|
| 1 | Most total fantasy points (season-long) |
| 2 | Head-to-head record among tied teams |
| 3 | Most wins in the season |
